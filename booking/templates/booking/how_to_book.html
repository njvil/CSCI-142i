Students' Reservation Procedure

  1. Check the availability of the facility.
  2. Fill out the reservation form.
  3. Secure the necessary endorsements (e.g., from faculty or the Office of Student Affairs, FSQA, FA, SOH, Comm, etc.).
  4. Submit your request for screening.
  5. Wait for the approval or disapproval of your request.

Reservation Lead Times

  • Simple Activities (e.g., classrooms): 3 Working Days.
  • Big Venues / Big Events (with logistics and AV equipment requirements): 5 Working Days.

Helpful Tips

To ensure you secure your desired venue, submit your booking requests as early as one month before the intended date of use.
